## Questions

1. Explain the **ACID properties** of a database transaction: Atomicity, Consistency, Isolation, and Durability. For each property, give a concrete real-world example from a banking or e-commerce system that shows what would go wrong if that property were violated.

Explain specifically what can go wrong when a multi-step business operation runs in autocommit mode. Use a concrete example like an order placement or a fund transfer.
